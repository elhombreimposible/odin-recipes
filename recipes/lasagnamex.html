Lasagna Mex

Mexican lasagna

Recreation of one of the oldest Italian pasta plates, but without pasta and with all the spicy flavor of the Mexican food. Easy to cook, great to eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet taco seasoning mix
  • 12 (8 inch) flour tortillas, or more if needed
  • 1 (14 ounce) can refried beans
  • 3 cups shredded Colby-Jack cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) jar taco sauce
  • 1 cup shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C and grease a casserole dish.
  2. Cook beef in a skillet for ten minutes. Add water and taco seasoning.
  3. Put tortillas and beans in the bottom of the casserole. Add a layer of beef and cheese. Repeat with other layer.
  4. Bake in the preheated for 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Turn on oven's broiler. Sprinkle 1 cup of cheese over lasagna
  6. Place lasagna under the broiler until the cheese is melted and bubbling, 3 to 5 minutes.
